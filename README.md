# Gaming Market Analysis Dashboard

A data analytics and business intelligence project focused on analyzing video game genre trends, platform performance, publisher success, and player versus critic ratings using dashboard visualization and statistical analysis.

This project was developed to generate data-driven insights for major gaming publishers such as Sony and Ubisoft to support strategic publishing and development decisions.

---

## Project Objective

The purpose of this analysis was to evaluate market trends within the gaming industry and identify factors influencing platform success, genre popularity, and game ratings.

The project focuses on answering key business questions such as:

* Which game genres dominate the market?
* Which platforms perform the best?
* How do critic and player ratings compare?
* Which publishers consistently release highly rated games?
* What strategic opportunities exist for future game development?

## Dashboard Analysis

### 1. Genre Performance Analysis

The analysis identified Action games as the dominant genre within the dataset, accounting for the largest share of releases. Genres such as Adventure, Puzzle, and Strategy showed significantly lower market representation.

#### Business Insight

Publishers may benefit from diversifying investment into underrepresented genres while incorporating successful gameplay and storytelling mechanics from high-performing Action titles.

---

### 2. Platform Performance Analysis

The dashboard revealed that PS4 contained the highest number of game releases and demonstrated strong player satisfaction consistency, while PS5 showed emerging growth potential.

#### Business Insight

Publishers should maintain the quality standards associated with successful PS4 releases while increasing genre diversity on PS5 to support long-term platform growth.

---

### 3. Critic vs Player Rating Analysis

The analysis compared Metacritic and PlayStation user scores to evaluate alignment between critic and player sentiment. Most games maintained stable scores between 82 and 90, indicating generally consistent product quality.

#### Business Insight

Publishers should balance critic expectations with player experience by incorporating user feedback into post-launch improvements and update strategies.

---

### 4. Publisher Performance Analysis

A comparative analysis between Sony Interactive Entertainment Europe and Ubisoft Entertainment SA demonstrated that both publishers maintain strong average review scores, although Sony consistently released a higher proportion of highly rated titles.

Inferential statistics and probability calculations were conducted using MATLAB, including Pearson and Spearman correlation analysis.

#### Business Insight

The findings suggest that publishers should prioritize innovation and gameplay differentiation rather than relying solely on review score competition.

---

## Key Findings

* Action games dominated overall market share.
* PS4 remained the most active and stable platform.
* Critics and players generally showed aligned rating patterns.
* Sony demonstrated strong consistency in highly rated releases.
* Smaller genres present potential growth opportunities for publishers.
